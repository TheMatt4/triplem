What is triplem.g6.cz ?

TripleM or Movie Moments Master is a tool for reviewing frames in any video by your mouse.

What videos to upload?

You can upload any video but it's recommended to choose 360p or 480p videos due to performace reasons. You can always set frame skipping for better performace.

Where is the video uploaded?

Nowhere. 'Upload' rather means select here. No data are leaving your browser.
